Children's Book · Illustration Papa and Piper Play Pinball

A children's book that follows a young bear named Piper as she discovers the world of pinball during a rainy-day outing with her dad.

Through playful illustrations and a lighthearted narrative, the story explores curiosity, perseverance, and the special bond that forms when we share the things we love with the people we care about.
