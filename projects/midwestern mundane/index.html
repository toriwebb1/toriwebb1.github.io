Photography · Series Midwestern Mundane

A photographic series bringing light to the quirky nostalgia and unassuming beauty of the true Midwest — the landscapes, roadside attractions, and small charms that shape my memories of home.

Inspired by the countless road trips of my childhood, I went looking for the unassuming beauty hiding in ordinary scenes. This series is my way of honoring the small, imperfect, and quietly meaningful moments that make the Midwest feel like home.
