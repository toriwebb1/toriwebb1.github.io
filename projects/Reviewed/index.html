Event Design · CGDC Reviewed

A portfolio review event hosted by the Chicago Graphic Design Club in partnership with Firebelly Design. Designed for open mentorship—bringing together designers and mentors—the event creates space for thoughtful feedback, conversation, and creative growth.

The event encouraged discussions that went beyond the portfolio itself, focusing on process, concept development, and the reasoning behind creative decisions. From shaping the event's structure to developing its visual identity, the goal was to create an approachable environment where designers could connect, share perspectives, and learn from one another.
