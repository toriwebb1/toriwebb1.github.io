Publication · Exhibition Made with Love

A collection of 50 recipes honoring the Southern cooking traditions carried through my family — a tribute to the women who taught me that love is something you cook.

I come from a long line of Southern women who expressed love through food — and this book is my way of carrying that tradition forward. Each recipe carries a memory tied to it, the book reads as much like a family album as it does a cookbook.

Results May Vary — LUMA Chicago

Exhibition

Made with Love was displayed at the Loyola University Museum of Art senior capstone showcase. The exhibition space included the capstone book, recipe cards made with ragcloth paper and featuring the custom typeface I created based from my grandmother's handwriting, quotes brought from my family, and other original recipe artifacts.
