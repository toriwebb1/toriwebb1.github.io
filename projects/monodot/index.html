Type Design · Animation monodot + monodule

A pair of experimental modular typefaces built from a single repeating dot or square — and brought to life in motion.

I wanted to see how far one small unit could be pushed, so I designed monodot and monodule around a grid of dots and squares that snap into letterforms. Animating them taught me that even the strictest systems create space for personality.
